VIN decoders translate a car's 17-character identification number into detailed specs including engine type, transmission, trim level, and factory options. MarketCheck's VIN decoding tool provides fast, accurate access to this data, helping prevent misrepresentation and improving decision-making for dealers, buyers, and analysts.
